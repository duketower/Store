# Reference Websites

Saved on: March 26, 2026

This is a shortlist of current reference websites from Australia and the United States that are useful for reviewing service positioning, page structure, proof presentation, pricing clarity, and premium company-site execution.

These are references for study, not templates to copy directly.

## Australia

### 1. Web App Studio

- Website: https://www.webappstudio.com.au/
- Why it matters:
  Strong overlap with Binary Ventures on websites, apps, POS, SEO, and business technology services.
- What to study:
  multi-service clarity, service framing, and how broad technical work is presented to business clients

### 2. FunnelBot Digital

- Website: https://funnelbotdigital.com.au/
- Why it matters:
  Strong reference for AI automation, lead systems, chatbots, voice workflows, and direct outcome-led copy.
- What to study:
  automation positioning, lead-gen language, and practical service packaging

### 3. Osher Digital

- Website: https://osher.com.au/
- Why it matters:
  Useful reference for turning automation and AI capability into business-oriented language instead of technical hype.
- What to study:
  case-study framing, services narrative, and trust-building structure

### 4. Webhouse

- Website: https://www.webhouse.dev/
- Why it matters:
  Strong modern premium-tech presentation with bold positioning and strong service confidence.
- What to study:
  visual direction, bold messaging, and premium studio tone

### 5. Appello Software

- Website: https://appello.com.au/
- Why it matters:
  Good reference for broader digital-product and custom-software presentation with polished multi-page structure.
- What to study:
  service hierarchy, enterprise-ready presentation, and project credibility
