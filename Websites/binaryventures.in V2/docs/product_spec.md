# Binary Ventures Website V2 Product Spec

## Goal

Position Binary Ventures as a premium, trusted studio that helps businesses set up the digital systems they actually need to run better.

## Audience

### Primary

- business owners
- operators
- growing teams
- US and Australian clients

### Strong Fit

- small and mid-sized businesses
- teams that need websites, internal systems, bots, or workflow automation

### Not Limited To

- the site should remain credible for broader custom technology work

## Core Offer

Complete business tech setup.

This includes:
- websites
- web apps
- bots
- automation
- operational systems such as POS or internal tools

## Product Requirements

- premium, high-trust feel
- multi-page structure
- concise homepage
- strong Work page
- anonymized proof where needed
- clear CTA paths to email or book a call

## Business Requirements

- communicate quality without sounding out of reach
- leave room for both package-style offers and custom solutions
- support later addition of maintenance or ongoing support offers
